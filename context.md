#### Gap Fee Calculation
**Example**: 60-minute session
- Fee charged: $250
- Medicare rebate (Item 80110): $141.85
- **Patient pays (gap)**: $108.15

### TAC (Transport Accident Commission)
- **Pre-approved**: Requires TAC claim number and approval
- **Rate**: Negotiated with TAC (typically $200-250/hour)
- **No patient gap**: TAC pays directly
- **Reports**: Separate fee schedule, must be pre-approved

### WorkSafe Victoria
- **Pre-approved**: Requires WorkSafe claim number and approval
- **Rate**: $250/hour (standard)
- **No patient gap**: WorkSafe pays directly
- **IME (Independent Medical Examinations)**: $300/hour minimum

### NDIS (National Disability Insurance Scheme)
- **Plan-managed or Self-managed**: Patient books, uses NDIS funds
- **Rate**: $193.99/hour (NDIS price guide 2024-25 for Level 2 Psychologist)
- **Clinical Psychologist**: May attract higher pricing tier (verify with plan)
- **No Medicare rebate**: Cannot claim both NDIS and Medicare

### Private Health Insurance
- Patient claims gap ($108.15) from their private health fund
- Rebate varies by fund and level of cover
- Clinical Psychologist status may increase rebate
